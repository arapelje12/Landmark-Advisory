Who We Are

A firm built for
government work.

Landmark Advisory is a Canadian consulting firm founded on a straightforward belief: that government departments deserve partners who understand their world — the procurement rules, the policy frameworks, the accountability structures, and the genuine complexity of delivering in the public interest.

Our practice spans technology strategy, policy research, organizational management, learning design, and strategic communications. We work across federal departments and agencies, bringing the same rigour to a plain language rewrite as to a departmental AI strategy.

We're based in Hamilton, Ontario, and operate as a fully Canadian-owned and operated company — positioned to meet Buy Canadian requirements and committed to building Canadian capacity in public sector consulting.

Established

Landmark Advisory Inc. is incorporated in Ontario, Canada, with a mandate focused entirely on federal public sector advisory services.

Location

Hamilton, Ontario — with capacity to work on-site across the National Capital Region and federal offices nationwide.

Procurement Registration

Registered on SAP Business Network (CanadaBuys), ProServices, and TBIPS/SBIPS supply arrangements. Procurement Business Number (PBN) issued.

Languages

Consulting services delivered in both official languages. English and French deliverables available for all practice areas.

Our Principles

What we stand for.

Four commitments that shape how we approach every engagement — from the first conversation to final delivery.

🎯

Outcomes over activity

We measure success by what clients can do with our work — not by hours billed or pages produced. Every deliverable is designed to be used, not filed.

📐

Uncompromising quality

Our work is reviewed against a high bar before it reaches a client. Accuracy, coherence, and strategic alignment are non-negotiable — always.

🤝

Genuine partnership

We work alongside our clients, not at arm's length. Accessible, responsive, and invested in the success of the mandate — not just the contract.

🔎

Intellectual rigour

We bring critical thinking to every file — questioning assumptions, stress-testing recommendations, and ensuring our advice reflects the best available evidence.
